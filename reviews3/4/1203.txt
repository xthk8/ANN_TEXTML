It is much better sound quality than my phone as it cancels out so much background noise and you don’t hear any wind. It also works seamlessly with my iPhone without need for adaptors or software to merge an audio clip with a video clip.At times I do wish it had better sound leveling/balance so if you are using this to record singing I wouldn’t recommend it, but it does really well for what I need - short clips for social media with speech/narration. It hasn’t been worth investing hundreds or thousands of dollars into a microphone package as I am just using speech
