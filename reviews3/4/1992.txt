These are something that is always good to have on hand, just in case. Over time, as with everything else, these tips will wear down, and its better to have backups then be stuck. Of course, using a regular glass protector or no protector at all, is the best way to ensure maximum life of your tips, but if you use something along the lines of Paperlike screen protectors, that will chew through your tips like there is no tomorrow. And of course, given its an official "Apple" product, it is overpriced for what you get, but if one is a purist (like myself), using a third party tip just will not cut it
